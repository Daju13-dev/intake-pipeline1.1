Frequently Asked Questions

Is my information shared with third parties?
We never sell your personal data, ID, or selfies to anyone. We only share information with trusted, heavily vetted service providers who help us run this specific program. These partners are bound by strict legal contracts and are only allowed to use your data exactly as we instruct them to for platform safety.
Is participation mandatory?
Not at all. Your participation is 100% voluntary. Choosing not to participate will not affect your ability to use our everyday services.
How secure is my data?
We treat your data with the highest level of security. From the moment you hit "submit," your information is protected by bank-grade encryption. It is stored in restricted, heavily monitored environments.
Can I withdraw my data?
Yes, you are always in control. If you change your mind, you can contact us to withdraw your consent. We will delete your personal records from our active systems.
